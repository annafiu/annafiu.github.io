sustainable residential masterplan proposal
green cluster housing

A masterplan proposal for an eco-conscious suburban residential cluster. The "green cluster" concept establishes a shared, central park space as the communal heart, with pedestrian-only paths connecting all homes without intersecting vehicular driveways.

The comprehensive study spans 24 residential units across types 60, 90, and 120, fully integrating a localized rainwater harvesting system and community-scaled solar arrays.
