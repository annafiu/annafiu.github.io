urban micro-housing conceptual study
micro housing study

A conceptual study focusing on micro-housing solutions within dense urban environments. Exploring compact living units ranging from 24 to 36m² that maintain high liveability standards through multi-functional custom furniture, high ceilings, and calculated spatial openings.

This design study generated three versatile unit typologies and a proposal for a 6-story building block featuring a communal rooftop space.
